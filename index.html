Memedhe Ibrahimi

Assistant Professor (RTDa) at Politecnico di Milano · DEIB · BONSAI Lab

DEIB — Politecnico di Milano

Milan, Italy

I am an Assistant Professor (RTDa) at Politecnico di Milano, within the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) and the BONSAI Lab. My research focuses on optical networking — including hollow-core fiber (HCF) deployment, the design of next-generation and 6G optical networks, and LLM-based network automation — and I teach data center networking.

I received my (industrial) Ph.D. from Politecnico di Milano in 2022. My doctoral thesis was recognized among the top-3 best Ph.D. theses in Telecommunications at DEIB, Politecnico di Milano.

Research interests: hollow-core fiber · optical network design · 6G optical infrastructure · data center networking · ML/LLMs for network automation.
