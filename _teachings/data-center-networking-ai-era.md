## Overview

**Level:** PhD &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Institution:** Politecnico di Milano

A graduate course on the design of data center networks in the era of AI, covering the data center networking landscape, optical transport and inter-data-center networking, communication collectives and RDMA/RoCE for large-scale AI training, and current research directions.
